Kwesi Baba

I'm a freelance photographer with close to 10 years experience shooting concerts, portraits, fashion shows and variety shows. Photography has been a love of mine from since I was little. My father entrusted the family camera to me to take shots of our parties and holidays. Now while it meant that I wasn't in a lot of those photos, I slowly learned basic concepts of photography and grew my interest. I have always been a visual storyteller, and photos allow me to capture the essence of so many beautiful stories over the years.
